Clover Configurator Pro

changelog

v2.0.0/2.0.1:

    • Major Update, totally written in swift 5.
    • Build Clover ability (edk2 master or UDK2018 at same time). New way to build Clover where the workspace is the patched one leaving edk2 (master) or UDK2018 totally vanilla. Patches_for_EDK2 or Patches_for_UDK2018 are infact applied on a separate sub directory giving you the possibility to leave the edk repository totally untouched but with Clover's patches up and running only for Clover v2. That also gives you the possibility to build other projects, or other UEFI bootloaders without the need of multiple and heavy copies of the tiano core's repository. Conflicts caused by old patches are just a bad memory.
    • Build HWMonitor-3 by Slice (with HWMonitorSMC2.app) for ardware monitoring.
    • Kexts installer with autodetection of the Clover's boot partition. Ability to download latest Lilu plugins or FakeSMC and plugins and other. You can install and repair permissions on any System-volume. The GUI provide an easy representaion of any kext installed into the System, plugins included.
    • The ESP mounter doesn't require any password.
    • The Plist Editor is aligned with all the new functionalities of Clover r4824+ .
    • Full Mojave compatibility.
    • Bug fixes for SVG Themes detection in v2.0.1.

v1.4.1:

    Fixed a known bug with Security framenwork  and AppKit in Sierra/El Capitan  (solved in 10.13) that was causing the Preferences' window to be blank. Solved by simply moving relevant code in viewDidAppear() to have a better timing setting constrains. Thanks to Philip Petev to show me the problem.

v1.3.8 (stable release):

    Fixed a bug selecting some options in ACPI->DSDT->Fixes.
